Retro Car Club

Welcome

We are your local classic car club catering for enthusiasts, owners or even just admirers of classic cars. We are an autonomous club and, as such, are designated 1020 (region 10 area 20). You do not have to be a member of the national club to join us, but there are many benefits and the club is very supportive of its members. In June each year, we hold a charity fund-raising event, in NY. All classic cars are welcome to park on the lawns of this Grade One listed Tudor manor house. From time to time we also make extra donations to other worthy causes.
